services

event planning, styling, creative consulting

  • whether its the whole 9 yards or only partial help, we can tailor a package together. birthday parties, baby showers, engagement parties, rehearsal dinners, bachelorette parties, bridal showers, weddings, & so on.

  • this budget friendly, DIY (do-it-yourself) service is for smaller, more casual parties & for the hands on person wants to do most everything themselves but wants party ingredients & instructions shipped to their door in a box.

  • an hourly service that allows complete freedom. perfect for event styling, creative consultation and virtual planning services.

    this service is commonly added on to the 'party in a box' DIY package but can used alone as well.

  • custom designs: invitations, menus, signs & beyond. create a one-of-a-kind invitations, custom menus, signage or other designs to use for your event.

    together with a graphic designer, i'll incorporate your event's theme into the designs to create a cohesive feel with original & innovative materials.

philosophy

    a bespoke approach

    my approach is always natural & guided by the underlying goal of creating a festive, easy-to-enjoy atmosphere that is aesthetically pleasing.

    an event of mine will never be like another event & couldn't be duplicated because all the parts naturally flow together & are unique to that particular event.

    style

    i would characterize my style as playfully elegant & innovative. i get things right without following a contrived prescription.

    it's hard to find someone who can throw an event that will feel so natural & easy, yet perfectly elegant, special & memorable.

    get lost in the details

    a lot of people telling you not to get lost in the details. i say ignore them. i ﻿tailor bespoke parties for each individual's vision, needs & personalities.

    my specialty is to get lost in the details. i'll plan your party with uniquely personal intricacies so that the event is a true representation of who you are.

    color outside of the lines

    im not scared to color outside the lines or try a wild or untraditional idea.

    at the end of the day, i cater towards the host's individual visons & quirks﻿.

    you don't need a big budget

    i'll tailor your event to your budget. i offer a mix of services that offer something for every budget, from "creativity consultations" with step-by-step DIY instructions, to event-day coordination, to an entire bespoke party.

    people often think that it costs a lot to throw a fabulous party but it simply doesn't have to.

    theme and overall vibe

    i always always start with a vision board & an underlying theme so that my events have a cohesive feel & organic flow.
